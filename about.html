About

Have you ever considered creating your own garden, but have been reluctant to waste your time or money if the plants are just going to die anyway?

The Casual Gardener was created to help anyone who is dreaming of starting their own garden -- whether you live in sunny Dallas or overcast Seattle, we can help! Simply select the city that is closest to where you live and then pick the plants you’d to grow in your simulated garden.

Keep an eye out for upgrades to our website, we’re always working to make your virtual garden even more realistic!
